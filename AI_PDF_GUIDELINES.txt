================================================================================
                 NUTRIBOX - AI CHATBOT PDF KNOWLEDGE BASE GUIDELINES
================================================================================

To ensure the RAG-based chatbot provides accurate and context-driven answers for your 
customers (especially when using the interactive suggestion wizard), your uploaded PDF 
should contain detailed information structured around the following categories. 

When you draft your PDF, make sure to cover these sections clearly:

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
1. DETAILED SUBSCRIPTION PLANS & TIERS
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Describe each box/tier with calorie estimates, portion targets, and ideal audiences:
* Classic Box: Balanced everyday meals (~500-600 kcal). Perfect for weight maintenance
  and general healthy eating. Includes standard vegetables and lean proteins.
* Premium Box: Chef-special ingredients (~600-750 kcal). Elevated culinary designs.
* Protein Rich Box: High-protein portions (~750-900 kcal, >40g protein). Designed 
  for muscle building, active gym-goers, and weight gain.
* Fruits Bowl: Fresh seasonal fruits, nuts, and seeds (~300-400 kcal). Ideal for detox,
  light snacks, or calorie-deficit weight loss plans.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
2. FITNESS GOALS ALIGNMENT (WEIGHT LOSS, GAIN, & MAINTENANCE)
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Add a section mapping fitness goals to specific plans to help the AI answer wizard queries:
* Weight Loss Guide:
  - Recommend the "Fruits Bowl" (for dinner slot) or "Classic Box" (Veg/Non-Veg).
  - Emphasize calorie deficits. Mention that Classic meals hover around 550 kcal.
  - Advise opting for "Veg Only" or "Lean Non-Veg" options.
* Weight Gain Guide:
  - Recommend the "Protein Rich Box" (both slots: Lunch & Dinner combo).
  - Emphasize calorie surplus and muscle recovery. Mention that Protein Rich meals 
    contain over 40g of protein and 800 kcal.
  - Advise adding healthy extras like nuts or extra egg whites.
* Weight Maintenance Guide:
  - Recommend the "Classic Box" (both slots or single slot depending on daily routine).

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
3. PRICING, BUNDLES & BUDGET BRACKETS
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Include a reference table for costs to align with the budget question:
* Weekly Package (Under ₹1000 - ₹2000 range):
  - Single Slot (Breakfast Only / Dinner Only) ranges from ₹900 to ₹1500 per week.
* Monthly Package (Over ₹2000 range):
  - Best value packages. Double Slot (Breakfast + Dinner) ranges from ₹3500 to ₹6000 
    depending on the tier selected.
* Delivery Charges:
  - Mention standard weekly shipping charges (e.g. ₹60) and monthly savings (e.g. ₹200).

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
4. CUSTOM PLANS & MENU CUSTOMIZATIONS
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Explain the custom plan submission policy for users asking to "gain this much" or 
"restrict specific ingredients":
* Process: Customers can submit requests via the "Custom Plan" tab in their portal.
* Options: Adjust macro ratios, exclude specific allergens (e.g. no gluten, no dairy,
  no onion/garlic), or target high-calorie counts.
* Review: A dietitian reviews the request within 24 hours, proposes a custom price,
  and once paid, the custom menu is sent to the kitchen.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
5. REFUND & CANCELATION POLICIES (SKIP CREDITS)
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
This is the most common customer question:
* Skip Deadline: Customers can skip any meal directly from their calendar.
  - Breakfast/Lunch skips must be made BEFORE 6:00 PM the previous day.
  - Dinner skips must be made BEFORE 10:00 AM the same day.
* Skip Credits: If skipped before the deadline, the meal cost is fully credited back
  to the user's account. This credit is automatically deducted from their next renewal.
* Subscription Cancelation: Subscription can be cancelled at any time. Active service
  ends immediately, and pending credits remain valid for future reactivations.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
SAMPLE TEMPLATE FOR A Q&A SECTION (RECOMMENDED)
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Including a direct Q&A section in the PDF makes RAG retrieval extremely precise:
- "Q: Which plan is best for reducing fat?
   A: The Fruits Bowl for snacks or the Classic Box (Veg) for lunch is recommended for calorie deficit and weight loss."
- "Q: Can I customize my meals?
   A: Yes, via the Custom Plan page. We accommodate allergen removals, macro targets, and special ingredient exclusions."
